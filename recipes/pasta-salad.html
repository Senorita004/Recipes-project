Pasta Salad

Description

This contains step by step instructions on how to prepare pasta salad.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta
  2. Combine the dressing and seasoning mix and whisk together
  3. Combine the pasta with the veegetables
  4. Add the seasoned dressing
